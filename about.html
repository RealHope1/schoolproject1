About Campus Navigator

Welcome to Campus Navigator, your essential guide to navigating our bustling university campus with ease. Designed with students, faculty, and visitors in mind, our application leverages the power of interactive mapping and intelligent routing to help you find your way around quickly and efficiently.

Know More About Campus Navigation

Easily find any campus location by name.

Key Features

  • Interactive Campus Map: Dynamic map showing all key buildings and facilities.
  • Smart Search: Find any campus location by name.
  • Turn-by-Turn Directions: Precise walking/driving directions.
  • "My Location" Feature: Instantly find your position.
  • Quick Links: Fast access to popular destinations.
  • Responsive Design: Works on any device.

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies for a smooth experience.

Our Technology

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Tailwind CSS
  • Mapping: Leaflet.js
  • Routing: OSRM & Nominatim
  • Font: Inter & Open Sans
