Welcome to Campus Navigator, your essential guide to navigating our bustling university campus with ease. Designed with students, faculty, and visitors in mind, our application leverages the power of interactive mapping and intelligent routing to help you find your way around quickly and efficiently.

Know More About Campus Navigation

Easily find any campus location by name.

Key Features

  • Interactive Campus Map: A dynamic map showing all key buildings, facilities, and points of interest.
  • Smart Search: Easily find any campus location by name.
  • Turn-by-Turn Directions: Get precise directions for walking, driving, or cycling between any two points on campus, powered by OSRM.
  • "My Location" Feature: Instantly pinpoint your current position on the map.
  • Quick Links: Fast access to popular campus destinations.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for seamless use on any device, from smartphones to desktops.

Technology Stack

Campus Navigator is built using modern web technologies to ensure a smooth and robust user experience:

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3 (Tailwind CSS for utility-first styling)
  • Mapping Library: Leaflet.js (lightweight, open-source JavaScript library)
  • Routing & Geocoding: Open Source Routing Machine (OSRM) and Nominatim (OpenStreetMap's geocoder) for directions and address lookup.
  • Font: Inter (for clean and modern typography).
